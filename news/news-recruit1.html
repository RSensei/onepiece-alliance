Recruiting Players!

Greetings! My name is KeiEs, I run a small YouTube Channel @KeiEsYoutube and I sometimes stream on Twitch at KeiEsTwitch. I created One Piece Alliance because I am huge fan of Commander in Magic:The Gathering and I love watching Shuffle up and play by the Professor over at Tolarian Community College. He inspired me to create my own channel and to come up with the Rules for One Piece Alliance.

I hope One Piece Alliance can become popular, because the two games I have played so far were very fun. The problem is, a format can't start if people don't play it! Here is where you come in: I want to play against you (either in person if you live in Japan or are visiting or Online over webcam) and record and post those games on my channel!

For content creators, or just anyone who wants to, you can introduce yourself and shoutout your own channels and pages, and of course you can upload your own edit of the gameplay if you choose to! It doesn't matter to me who gets the views as long as more people find out about this wonderful new way to play! Of course, you can also stay annoymous if you like to just be in the gameplay video.

If you are interested, please send me an email at KeiEs.Content@gmail.com with the subject line:
"One Piece Alliance - let's play!"
and lets work out the details from there!
