One Piece Alliance

A new way to play One Piece Card Game

Alliance is a new format created by KeiEs that takes inspiration from Magic: The Gathering's Commander format. It is played with mostly the same rules as the normal One Piece Card game we know and love, with a few special twists. Click the buttons below to learn more!

Overview

Two leaders

Each players starts with two Leader cards, one of which resides in the DON!! area.

Singleton

A deck may not contain more than one copy of each card (unless the card says otherwise).

Backup Leader

The leader that is placed in the DON!! area has three special effects and can be switched with the Main Leader during the game.

Colors

Your deck may contain cards from the color(s) of either of your Leaders.
